Privacy Policy — FreeZone Pro

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

FreeZone Pro (“the App”) is a business comparison application designed to help users compare UAE Free Zones. Your privacy is respected by design.

1. Personal Information Collection

FreeZone Pro does not collect any personal information.

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers
  • No forms or user input storage

The app does not collect any information from your device such as location, contacts, camera data, or files.

2. Automatically Collected Information

FreeZone Pro does not collect any automatically generated data, including:

  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics
  • Crash logs
  • Analytics data

3. Tools, Services, and Third Parties

FreeZone Pro does not use any third-party tools or services, including analytics, advertising networks, payment processors, remarketing, or CAPTCHA services.

No data is shared with third parties.

4. Advertising

FreeZone Pro does not display ads and does not use Advertising ID.

5. Payments

FreeZone Pro does not offer paid features, subscriptions, or in-app purchases.

6. Children’s Privacy

FreeZone Pro is intended for users aged 18 and above. The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Legal Compliance Statements

Since FreeZone Pro does not collect, store, or process any personal data, the app does not require GDPR/CCPA/CalOPPA data-handling disclosures.

8. Data Security

Because no user data is collected, there is no personal data stored or transmitted. Any network communication uses secure HTTPS by default.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in the future and data collection is introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
